Here are some fun facts about cat food that you might find interesting:

  • Cats are obligate carnivores: They require a diet high in animal protein to thrive.
  • Flavor preferences: Cats have a keen sense of smell and can detect flavors differently than humans, often preferring savory tastes.
  • Water intake: Cats can be prone to dehydration; feeding them wet food can help increase their water intake.
